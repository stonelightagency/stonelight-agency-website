10 Songs You Need for Your Ceremony (That Aren’t Canon in D)

Published on May 23, 2025 by Keys by Caleb

Pachelbel's Canon in D is a classic for a reason, but your wedding ceremony is a chance to express your unique style. If you're looking for something a little different, yet equally beautiful and moving, consider these alternatives for your processional, interlude, or recessional moments. Live piano arrangements of these pieces can add an incredibly personal and elegant touch.

For a Romantic & Ethereal Entrance:

  1. "A Thousand Years" by Christina Perri: A modern favorite that translates beautifully to piano, evoking timeless romance.
  2. "River Flows in You" by Yiruma: A delicate and popular contemporary piano piece that’s both soothing and emotional.
  3. "Glasgow Love Theme" from Love Actually: Instantly recognizable and incredibly romantic, perfect for a cinematic feel.
  4. "Concerning Hobbits" from Lord of the Rings: Whimsical, heartwarming, and perfect for a slightly unconventional, joyful processional.

For a Joyful & Uplifting Exit:

  1. "Signed, Sealed, Delivered I'm Yours" by Stevie Wonder: An upbeat and classic choice for a happy recessional.
  2. "This Will Be (An Everlasting Love)" by Natalie Cole: Pure joy and celebration, perfect for your first steps as a married couple.
  3. "You Are the Best Thing" by Ray LaMontagne: A soulful and heartfelt option for a memorable exit.

Modern & Unique Choices:

  1. Instrumental covers of your favorite Indie/Folk songs: Think Bon Iver, The Lumineers, or Florence + The Machine, beautifully arranged for solo piano.
  2. Theme from "Up" (Married Life): Sweet, poignant, and wonderfully unique for a processional or special moment.
  3. "Storybook Love" from The Princess Bride: A charming and whimsical choice that evokes fairytale romance.

Remember, the beauty of live piano is its versatility. Many songs can be adapted to suit the tone and pacing of your ceremony. Don't be afraid to discuss your favorite music with your pianist – you might be surprised what can be transformed into a stunning ceremony piece!
