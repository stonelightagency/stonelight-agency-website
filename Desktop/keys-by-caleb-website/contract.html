ARTICLE III
TERMS AND CONDITIONS

3.1 Cancellation. If the Client cancels this Agreement at any time after execution, the retainer shall be forfeited. If cancellation occurs within thirty (30) days of the Event Date, the full contracted fee shall be due to the Artist. If the Artist cancels, he shall make every effort to secure a suitable replacement, subject to Client approval. If no replacement is found, all monies paid, including the retainer, shall be refunded to the Client.

3.2 Postponement. The Client may postpone the Event once without penalty, subject to the Artist's availability. The retainer will transfer to a new date within 12 months. Subsequent postponements incur a $100 fee.

3.3 Cooperation and Conduct. The Parties agree to cooperate in good faith. The Artist is not liable for performance issues arising from event delays, venue restrictions, or lack of cooperation from the Client or other vendors. The Artist reserves the right to a safe working environment and may terminate performance if their safety is compromised, without refund.

3.4 Technical Requirements. The Client or Venue shall provide a level 5'x6' space, access to a standard power outlet, and one professional powered speaker with a standard input. For outdoor events, adequate shelter from the elements is required.

3.5 Copyright. The Artist retains all copyright to any and all musical recordings created. The Client is granted a limited license for personal use only and may not sell, alter, or use the recordings for commercial purposes without written permission.
