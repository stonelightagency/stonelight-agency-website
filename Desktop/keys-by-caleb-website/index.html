Crafting Your Perfect Moment

Live piano is more than just background music; it's the soundtrack to your memories. Discover the experience I can create for your event.

The Ceremony

Imagine walking down the aisle to a beautiful, flowing piano arrangement of your chosen song. I provide elegant music for the prelude, processional, and recessional to create a truly romantic and unforgettable atmosphere.

Cocktail & Dinner Hour

Set a sophisticated and welcoming tone for your guests. From classic jazz standards to contemporary pop, I curate a playlist that complements conversation and enhances the ambiance of your reception or event.

Your Special Song

Have a song that's uniquely yours? Playing primarily by ear allows me to learn new pieces quickly. I'll work with you to create a personalized arrangement for your first dance, parent dance, or any other special moment.

About Me

Your Event Pianist

Hi, I'm Caleb! For 10 years, the piano has been my passion. Based in Riverside, I specialize in bringing elegant live music to weddings and special events, drawing on extensive experience including over three years playing for live worship services.

Playing primarily by ear allows me to learn new pieces quickly – perfect for special requests! I enjoy collaborating closely with clients to curate a personalized musical backdrop that truly complements your event's unique ambiance.
