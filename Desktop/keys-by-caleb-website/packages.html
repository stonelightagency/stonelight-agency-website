Service Packages

Find the perfect live piano experience for your wedding or special event.

Just One Moment Package

$495

This package is designed for those key, singular moments that deserve a dedicated musical touch. Ideal for wedding ceremonies, intimate proposals, or a short, impactful musical interlude at any event.

What's Included:

  • Duration: Up to 1 hour of continuous live piano music.

  • Ideal Applications: Perfect for wedding ceremonies (including prelude, processional, recessional, interludes), wedding proposals, elopements, vow renewals, short cocktail/dinner segments, or memorial services.

  • Personalized Song Selection: Assistance with song selection is provided to perfectly match your event's mood and style.

  • Custom Song Requests: Option to learn special song requests not already in my repertoire (subject to approval and advance notice).

  • Equipment Provision: Professional digital piano and necessary cables are provided. Client/Venue must provide one (1) powered speaker.

  • Vendor Coordination: Seamless coordination with your event planner, officiant, or other vendors to ensure perfect timing.

  • Travel Inclusion: Includes travel within a 30-mile radius of Riverside, CA.

Best For:

  • Focused Moments: Ideal for clients who need elegant live music for a specific, impactful part of their event without requiring extended coverage.

  • Concise Events: Perfect for elopements, intimate proposals, or segments of larger events like short cocktail hours or dinner interludes.

The Full Experience Package

Starting at $1495

Our most popular package, 'The Full Experience' offers comprehensive live piano coverage for your entire wedding day or multi-segment event, ensuring a seamless and elegant musical flow from start to finish.

What's Included:

  • Duration: Up to 5 hours of continuous live piano music coverage for your event.

  • Comprehensive Coverage: Includes music for multiple segments such as ceremony (prelude, processional, recessional, interludes), cocktail hour, dinner reception, and special dances (first dance, parent dances).

  • Complimentary Vinyl Keepsake: Receive a custom-pressed vinyl record of a special song from your wedding as a timeless memento ($275 value).

  • Bonus Rehearsal Dinner Music: Includes up to 1 hour of complimentary background music for your rehearsal dinner, setting the perfect tone for the weekend ($150 value).

  • Detailed Planning Consultation: Extensive one-on-one consultation to meticulously plan your musical timeline and preferences.

  • Custom Song Requests: Option to learn special song requests not already in my repertoire (subject to approval and advance notice).

  • My Equipment: Professional digital piano and necessary cables are provided.

  • Required from Venue: Client/Venue must provide one (1) powered speaker with a standard input to ensure crystal-clear audio.

  • Full Vendor Coordination: Proactive and seamless coordination with all relevant vendors (wedding planner, venue staff, officiant, photographer, etc.).

  • Travel Inclusion: Includes travel within a 50-mile radius of Riverside, CA.

Best For:

  • Full Wedding Days: Designed for couples planning a comprehensive wedding day who desire continuous, elegant live music from ceremony through reception.

  • Multi-Segment Events: Ideal for corporate events, galas, or large private parties with distinct phases requiring consistent musical ambiance.

Hourly Performance Rate

$295 / Hour (2-hour minimum)

Flexible live piano music booked by the hour, perfect for events with specific duration needs, or for functions that don't fit into a standard package. Minimum 2 hours required.

What's Included:

  • Flexible Duration: Live piano performance for a minimum of 2 consecutive hours, with the flexibility to book additional time as needed.

  • Ideal Applications: Perfect for private parties, corporate receptions, galas, elopements, small ceremonies requiring more than one hour, background music for restaurants/venues, or holiday gatherings.

  • Music Consultation: Consultation provided to discuss your desired musical style and ambiance for the event.

  • Equipment: Professional digital piano and necessary cables are provided. Client/Venue must provide one (1) powered speaker.

  • Travel Inclusion: Includes travel within a 30-mile radius of Riverside, CA.

Best For:

  • Events with Specific Durations: Clients who need flexible music coverage for events of varying lengths, precisely matching their timeline.

  • Unique Requirements: Functions that don't fit into a standard package, offering a tailored approach to musical needs.

Optional Enhancements

Elevate your event with these unique, memorable add-ons. Each can be combined with any of our primary packages to create a truly bespoke musical experience.

Custom Pressed Vinyl Record

  • The Ultimate Keepsake: Immortalize the music from your special day on a beautiful, custom-pressed vinyl record. We capture the live arrangements from your wedding and turn them into a timeless heirloom you can listen to for years to come.

  • Personalized Design: Includes custom-designed artwork for the sleeve and label, with options for stunning marble or colored vinyl to match your wedding's aesthetic.

  • Pricing: $275 for a single track (e.g., your processional), or starting from $495 - $650 for a full wedding set compilation.

Rehearsal Dinner Performance

  • Set the Tone: Set a relaxed, sophisticated, and welcoming atmosphere for your pre-wedding celebration. Live piano provides the perfect, non-intrusive backdrop for conversation and mingling as your families and friends come together.

  • Pricing: A special rate of $150 - $200 when added to an existing wedding package. Also available as a standalone booking at our standard hourly rate.

Spoken Dedication Over Music

  • A Heartfelt Message: A truly personal and emotional touch. You, your parents, or a loved one can pre-record a heartfelt message, tribute, or reading. I then compose and record a beautiful, original piano arrangement to play underneath the spoken words, creating a powerful moment for your ceremony or reception.

  • Pricing: $150 per recorded message. Includes professional audio mixing of the voice and music.

Spotify Playlist & Physical Keepsake

  • Your Soundtrack, To Go: Take the sounds of your wedding home. Receive a curated Spotify playlist featuring the key songs from your event, including links to my own recordings where available. This comes with a chic physical display piece: a glass/acrylic plaque with an etched Spotify code, set on a handsome wooden base.

  • Pricing: Starting at $125. A perfect thank-you gift for parents or a beautiful memento for your own home.

Custom Inquiries & Collaborations

Have a unique musical vision or an event that doesn't quite fit our standard packages? We love bringing custom musical ideas to life! Reach out to discuss your specific needs.

This Option Is For:

  • Unique Event Requirements: For events with highly specific or unusual musical requirements that fall outside typical package structures.

  • Collaborations: Opportunities to collaborate with other musicians, artists, or entertainers to create a richer musical experience.

  • Extended Engagements: Ideal for multi-day events, recurring performances, or residencies at venues.

  • Extensive Customization: For projects requiring learning an extensive list of new or highly specialized custom song arrangements.

  • Extended Travel: Events located significantly outside our standard travel radius in Riverside, CA.

  • Any Creative Project: If you have any other creative musical project or unique vision in mind, we're eager to discuss how to bring it to fruition.

The Process:

  • Initial Consultation: We'll begin with a detailed discussion about your vision, specific event details, and musical preferences.

  • Custom Proposal: Following our consultation, you will receive a tailored proposal outlining the bespoke services, pricing, and logistical plan.

  • Confirmation & Collaboration: Upon your approval, we'll confirm the booking and collaborate closely to prepare and execute your unique musical experience flawlessly.
